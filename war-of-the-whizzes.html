About the Event

What is War of the Whizzes?

War of the Whizzes is a fast-paced knowledge battle where quick thinking and data intuition are your only weapons.

Teams will compete in multiple rapid-fire rounds covering a vast range of topics from data trends to technological history. It's a test of both breadth of knowledge and the speed of retrieval. Only the smartest and quickest will survive this intellectual battlefield.

Quiz / Strategy 1 to 3 Dynamic Difficulty Mini Event

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participation allowed individually or in teams of up to 3.
  • All participants must be currently enrolled students.
  • The quiz consists of multiple elimination rounds.
  • Use of mobile phones or any external resources is strictly forbidden.
  • In case of a tie, a sudden-death round will determine the winner.
  • The quizmaster's word is final.
