Data Meme War

Where data meets humor.

About the Event

What is Data Meme War?

Data Meme War is a fast-paced creativity showdown where participants turn data science struggles, coding chaos, and analytics situations into original, relatable memes that resonate with the tech community.

Submit your most creative and hilarious memes, and let the audience and judges decide who wins the internet. No coding skills required — just a great sense of humor and a passion for data culture.

Creative / Fun 1-3 Open to All Levels Pre-Fest Event

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual participation only — no team submissions.
  • Each participant may submit a maximum of 3 memes.
  • Memes must be original and created for this event — no reposts.
  • Content must be data science, AI, coding, or analytics themed.
  • No offensive, political, or inappropriate content will be accepted.
  • Accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF — max 5 MB per file.
  • Judging criteria: Creativity, Relevance, Humor, and Originality.
  • Organiser's decision on results is final.
