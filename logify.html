About the Event

What is Logify?

Logify is a fast-paced brand recognition challenge that tests your visual memory, speed, and attention to detail under pressure. Participants decode blurred and altered logos across multiple rounds, combining sharp observation with quick thinking. It’s not just about spotting the brand—it’s about how fast and accurately you can crack it

Logic / Puzzles 1 to 3 High Difficulty Mini Event

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participation: Teams of 1 to 3 members (Same college only).
  • Requirement: Participants must bring their own laptop.
  • Format: Rounds include 'Guess the Logo' (blurred, partial, or taglines).
  • Details: Identify brand owners, CEOs, and handle bonus rounds.
  • Duration: 1 Hour of intense logical challenges.
  • Strictly no internet, mobile phones, or external resources.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
