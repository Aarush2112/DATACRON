About the Event

What is Logic Labs?

Logic Labs is a challenging pre-fest event designed to test your logical reasoning, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities through multiple rounds of progressively harder challenges.

From pattern recognition to lateral thinking puzzles, every round is crafted to push you beyond conventional thought. Whether you work solo or partner up, only the sharpest minds advance to the final.

Logic / Problem Solving 1 to 4 Medium-High Difficulty Pre-Fest Event

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participation allowed individually or in teams of 4.
  • All participants must be currently enrolled students.
  • Multiple rounds — elimination after each based on score.
  • No use of internet, calculators, or AI tools during rounds.
  • Questions will test logical, pattern-based, and lateral thinking.
  • Answers must be submitted before the timer ends — late answers are void.
  • In case of a tie, a tiebreaker round will decide the winner.
  • Organiser's decision on scoring and rankings is final.
